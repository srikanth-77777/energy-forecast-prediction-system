===== DESIGN REQUIREMENTS =====
- White background (for research paper printing)
- Clean sans-serif font (Arial or Helvetica)
- Each layer clearly separated with distinct pastel background colors
- Arrows should be solid black with labels
- Sub-components should have thin borders
- Use consistent rounded rectangles for processes, cylinders for storage, diamonds for decision points
- Document shapes for CSV files
- The diagram should be wide (landscape orientation, approximately 16:9 aspect ratio)
- Resolution should be high enough for IEEE two-column format (minimum 300 DPI)
- Keep it professional and technical — no cartoonish elements
